Welcome Habit Streak!

Habit Streak is the easiest way to keep track of your most important daily habits. Follow these instructions to get set up:

  1. Open the settings window in the top right.
  2. Fill out the information to specify what time you'd like to receive texts, what number, and what you'd like to keep track of.
  3. You can respond to texts with a simple "Y" or "N" depending on whether or not you've met your goals for the day.
  4. Log back in to the app any time you'd like to check on your progress.
